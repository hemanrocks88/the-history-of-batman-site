Fan Creations

Fans express their love for Batman through various mediums, including fan art, videos, and cosplay. One notable example is Michael Hills, a 22-year-old from Jacksonville, who has become a social media sensation by portraying Batman. In just three years, Hills has gained over 1.2 million followers on TikTok and Instagram by uploading videos where he dons a suit similar to Robert Pattinson's portrayal of Batman in 2022's "The Batman." :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}
